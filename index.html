A staple loved across the Caribbean & Latin America

Casabe is one of the oldest breads of the Americas — crisp, mild and versatile. Enjoyed on its own, toasted, with soup or coffee, or as a base for cheese and avocado. Peña "Casabe del Caribe" is the original, authentic recipe, exported from Santiago, Dominican Republic.
